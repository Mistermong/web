BIO



Mr. Mong (1982) lives and works in Ghent.
When he was a young kid. Anything that crossed his path that could make him draw, we're doomed.
He's always busy on studying, practicing and executing illustrations of all sizes.
If not, he's on a coffee-break.

“Whether it’s a tiny drawing or a painting on a huge wall... It’s always a challenge to put the lines correct” mumbles the man.

Humor. Horror. Monkeys. Girls with Curls. Rock ‘n’ Roll.
Those are his favorite ingredients that could serve you an exclusive Mr. Mong cocktail to sip from.
